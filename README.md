## Advance Group of Companies — Family Business

**[Advance Group of Companies (AGC)](https://agc-green.com/)** is a multi-subsidiary engineering and renewable energy group established in 1997, headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan. The group operates across engineering, solar, EV charging, and renewable energy sectors with both local and international clients.

I have supported the group's finance function from an early age, initially working alongside the company accountant during my school years and continuing to contribute to financial operations through to today.

**What I have done:**

- Assisted with **petty cash management** — recording daily cash transactions, maintaining the petty cash log, and performing periodic cash counts and reconciliations
- **Generated and processed invoices** for clients and suppliers — ensuring accuracy, correct VAT treatment, and timely dispatch
- Supported **accounts payable** — tracking outstanding supplier invoices, processing payments, and maintaining supplier records
- Supported **accounts receivable** — monitoring outstanding client payments, following up on overdue accounts, and maintaining debtor records
- Assisted with **budgeting and financial planning** — supporting the preparation of budgets and monitoring actual vs. budgeted spend
- Contributed to **financial reporting** — helping prepare internal statements and summaries for management review
